# Sample credits

All instrument samples in this folder are per-note renders of the
**FluidR3 General MIDI soundfont** by Frank Wen, released under the
**MIT license**, obtained from the
[gleitz/midi-js-soundfonts](https://github.com/gleitz/midi-js-soundfonts)
project (also MIT).

| Folder | FluidR3 GM instrument |
|---|---|
| `bass/` | Electric Bass (finger) |
| `bassp/` | Electric Bass (pick) |
| `keys/` | Electric Piano 1 |
| `pad/` | Pad 2 (warm) |
| `guitar/` | Acoustic Guitar (steel) |
| `eguitar/` | Electric Guitar (clean) |
| `nylon/` | Acoustic Guitar (nylon) |

FluidR3 (c) Frank Wen; MP3 renders via midi-js-soundfonts (c) Benjamin Gleitzman.
Both are MIT-licensed: free to use, modify, and redistribute with this notice.
Drum sounds in GuitarLab remain synthesized in Web Audio (no samples).
